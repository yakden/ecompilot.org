## Overview

The Barcode Scanner lets you enter any EAN or UPC barcode and instantly pulls product data from Icecat, UPC Database, and Open Food Facts. It auto-fills listing cards with brand, category, and nutrition data (for food products), saving you hours of manual data entry. The mobile app adds camera-based scanning powered by Expo Camera for on-the-go product lookups.

## Key Features

- **Multi-source lookup** — queries Icecat, UPC Database, and Open Food Facts simultaneously to maximize data coverage.
- **Auto-fill listing cards** — populates brand, product name, category, weight, dimensions, and other attributes directly into your listing drafts.
- **Nutrition data for food products** — Open Food Facts integration returns ingredients, allergens, and nutrition tables for food and beverage items.
- **Camera-based scanning** — the mobile app uses Expo Camera to scan physical barcodes, no manual typing needed.
- **EAN and UPC support** — works with both European (EAN-13) and North American (UPC-A) barcode formats.
- **Scan history** — previously scanned products are saved for quick reference.

## How It Works

1. Open the **Barcode Scanner** from the dashboard or mobile app.
2. Enter an EAN or UPC code manually, or tap the camera icon in the mobile app to scan a physical barcode.
3. The system queries Icecat, UPC Database, and Open Food Facts in parallel and aggregates the results.
4. Review the returned product data — brand, category, description, images, and (for food) nutrition information.
5. Use the auto-fill button to push this data into a new or existing listing card.
6. Edit any field before saving if you need to adjust descriptions or categories for your marketplace.
