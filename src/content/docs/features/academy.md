## Overview

Academy is EcomPilot's learning hub for Polish marketplace sellers. Watch structured video courses covering Allegro selling, Amazon Poland, importing from China, and more. Courses range from beginner to advanced, each ending with a certificate you can showcase on your profile. Track your progress, see how many students have completed each course, and build the skills you need to grow your business.

## Key Features

- **Structured video courses** — professionally produced lessons organized into modules with clear learning objectives.
- **Topic coverage** — Allegro selling fundamentals, Amazon Poland launch strategy, importing from China, pricing tactics, listing optimization, and more.
- **Beginner to advanced levels** — courses are tagged by difficulty so you always know what matches your experience.
- **Certificates upon completion** — finish all lessons in a course to earn a shareable certificate.
- **Progress tracking** — see enrolled courses, completed lessons, total duration, and where you left off.
- **Student count and ratings** — each course shows how many students enrolled and their average rating to help you choose.

## How It Works

1. Open **Academy** from the main navigation.
2. Browse available courses or filter by topic, level, or duration.
3. Enroll in a course to add it to your learning dashboard.
4. Watch video lessons in order. Each lesson includes a summary and optional quiz.
5. Track your progress across all enrolled courses — lesson count, time spent, and percentage complete.
6. Complete all lessons in a course to unlock your certificate.
