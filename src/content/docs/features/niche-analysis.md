## Overview

Niche Analysis helps you find profitable product categories on Allegro by scoring competition density, demand trends, and margin potential. Every category receives a NicheScore from 0 to 100, giving you a clear, data-driven signal on where to invest your time and capital. Filter results by category and price range to zero in on opportunities that match your business model.

## Key Features

- **NicheScore 0-100** — a composite rating combining competition, demand, and margin signals into a single actionable number.
- **Competition density analysis** — see how many active sellers operate in a category and how concentrated sales are among top performers.
- **Demand trend tracking** — monitor search volume and sales velocity changes over time to spot rising and declining niches.
- **Margin potential estimation** — approximate gross margin based on average selling prices, typical sourcing costs, and Allegro fees.
- **Category and price range filters** — narrow results to categories and price brackets that fit your strategy.
- **Competitor monitoring** — track specific sellers and get notified when they enter or exit a niche (Pro and above).

## How It Works

1. Open the **Niche Analysis** tool from the dashboard.
2. Select a top-level Allegro category or enter a keyword to search across categories.
3. Optionally set a price range filter (e.g., 50–200 PLN) to focus on a specific segment.
4. The system calculates the NicheScore for each matching category by pulling competition, demand, and margin data.
5. Review the ranked list of niches. Click any row to drill into detailed metrics — seller count, average price, estimated monthly sales, and trend charts.
6. On Pro and Business plans, add competitors to your monitoring list to receive alerts on pricing and assortment changes.
