## Overview

The Community Forum is where Polish e-commerce sellers share knowledge, rate suppliers, and discuss strategy. With AI-powered moderation and a reputation system, conversations stay high-quality and spam-free. Browse 10 categories covering everything from success stories to legal advice, and tap into the collective experience of thousands of marketplace sellers.

## Key Features

- **10 discussion categories** — Success Stories, Suppliers, Strategy, Listings, Legal & Finance, Logistics, Marketplaces, Tools & Automation, Beginner Questions, and Off-Topic.
- **AI moderation** — automated content filtering removes spam, self-promotion, and low-effort posts to keep discussions useful.
- **Reputation system** — earn points for helpful posts, accepted answers, and community upvotes. Higher reputation unlocks badges and visibility.
- **Post, reply, and like** — standard forum interactions with threaded replies and notification support.
- **Hot and recent sorting** — switch between trending discussions and chronological view to find what matters most.
- **Supplier ratings** — community members rate suppliers based on quality, reliability, and communication. Ratings aggregate into a public score.
- **Strategy ratings** — vote on selling strategies and tactics to surface the approaches that actually work.

## How It Works

1. Open the **Community Forum** from the main navigation.
2. Browse categories or use the search bar to find discussions on a specific topic.
3. Switch between **Hot** (most active and upvoted) and **Recent** (newest first) sorting.
4. Click into a thread to read the full discussion. Reply with your own experience or upvote helpful answers.
5. Create a new post by selecting a category, writing your content, and publishing. AI moderation reviews the post automatically.
6. Build reputation over time through quality contributions. Your reputation score appears on your profile and next to every post.
