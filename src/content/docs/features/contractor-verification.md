## Overview

Contractor Verification lets you validate any Polish or EU business partner before you start working together. Enter a NIP (Polish tax identification number) and instantly pull data from four official registries — VIES, KRS, CEIDG, and REGON. The tool displays company name, registered address, VAT status (active or invalid), and registration details so you can avoid questionable suppliers and protect your business.

## Key Features

- **VIES (VAT Information Exchange System)** — confirm EU VAT registration status for cross-border transactions.
- **KRS (National Court Register)** — look up companies registered in the Polish court register, including board members and financial filings.
- **CEIDG (Central Register of Business Activity)** — verify sole proprietorships and micro-businesses registered in Poland.
- **REGON (National Business Registry)** — retrieve statistical identification numbers and company classification codes.
- **Instant NIP lookup** — enter a 10-digit NIP and get results in seconds from all available registries.
- **VAT status indicator** — clearly shows whether a company is an active VAT payer or has been deregistered.
- **Fraud protection** — flag suppliers with inconsistent data across registries or recently suspended VAT status.

## How It Works

1. Open **Contractor Verification** from the dashboard or the supplier detail page.
2. Enter the NIP (tax ID) of the company you want to verify.
3. The system queries VIES, KRS, CEIDG, and REGON in parallel.
4. Review the consolidated report showing company name, legal form, registered address, VAT status, and registration dates.
5. If the company is flagged (e.g., inactive VAT, missing from expected registries), a warning banner appears with details.
6. Save the verification result to your records for future reference.
