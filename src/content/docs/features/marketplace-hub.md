## Overview

Marketplace Hub is your central command for multi-channel selling. Connect up to eight marketplaces — Allegro, Amazon, eBay, Etsy, OLX, Vinted, Empik, and Erli — and manage all your listings, pricing, and reviews from one interface. Auto-listing creation, price optimization, and cross-platform review management eliminate the need to switch between seller dashboards.

## Key Features

- **8 marketplace integrations** — Allegro, Amazon, eBay, Etsy, OLX, Vinted, Empik, and Erli connected through official APIs.
- **Unified listing management** — create, edit, pause, and delete listings across all platforms from a single view.
- **Auto-listing creation** — define a product once and let EcomPilot generate platform-specific listings automatically.
- **Price optimization** — AI analyzes competitor pricing and suggests optimal prices per platform to maximize margin and sales velocity.
- **Review management** — monitor and respond to customer reviews across all connected marketplaces in one feed.
- **Inventory sync** — keep stock levels consistent across platforms to prevent overselling.
- **Performance dashboard** — track sales, views, and conversion rates per platform and per product.

## How It Works

1. Navigate to **Marketplace Hub** and connect your seller accounts via OAuth or API key.
2. Import existing listings or create new products in the unified product catalog.
3. For each product, select which marketplaces to publish on. EcomPilot adapts titles, descriptions, and images to each platform's requirements.
4. Enable **Price Optimizer** to receive pricing suggestions based on competitor data and your margin targets.
5. Monitor incoming reviews from all platforms in the **Reviews** tab. Reply directly from EcomPilot.
6. Use the performance dashboard to compare sales across channels and identify your strongest marketplaces.
