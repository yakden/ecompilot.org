## Overview

Legal Guides is a library of 30+ articles covering the legal, tax, and regulatory topics that Polish e-commerce sellers encounter most often. Topics range from JDG sole proprietorship registration and VAT obligations to GDPR compliance, trademark protection, and customs procedures. All guides are available in four languages — Polish, English, Russian, and Ukrainian — with searchable content, category filters, and FAQ sections.

## Key Features

- **30+ topics** — JDG registration, VAT rules, ZUS social insurance, flat tax (ryczalt), trademarks, GDPR, customs duties, Allegro strategy, product compliance, and managing customer reviews.
- **4 languages** — every guide is available in Polish (PL), English (EN), Russian (RU), and Ukrainian (UA).
- **Searchable library** — full-text search across all guides to find answers fast.
- **Category filters** — browse by topic area: tax, legal, compliance, strategy, or logistics.
- **FAQ per guide** — each article includes a frequently asked questions section addressing the most common follow-ups.
- **Reading time estimates** — see how long each guide takes to read before you start.
- **Regular updates** — guides are revised when regulations change so the information stays current.

## How It Works

1. Open **Legal Guides** from the main navigation or sidebar.
2. Browse by category or use the search bar to find a specific topic (e.g., "VAT registration" or "GDPR").
3. Select your preferred language using the language toggle at the top of any guide.
4. Read the guide — each one follows a structured format: introduction, step-by-step explanation, practical tips, and FAQ.
5. Use the FAQ section at the bottom for quick answers to common questions.
6. Check the "Last updated" date to confirm the guide reflects current regulations.
