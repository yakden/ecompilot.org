## Overview

The Supplier Database gives you a curated, verified directory of manufacturers and wholesalers across China, Poland, Turkey, and the EU. Every supplier profile includes response time, shipping days to Poland, supported platforms, languages spoken, and dropshipping availability. Community ratings and reviews from real EcomPilot users help you pick partners you can trust.

## Key Features

- **21+ verified suppliers** on the free tier, expanding to 200+ on Pro and Business plans.
- **Community ratings and reviews** — read first-hand feedback from other Polish sellers who have worked with each supplier.
- **Detailed supplier profiles** — response time, average shipping days to Poland, MOQ (minimum order quantity), supported e-commerce platforms, languages, and dropshipping availability.
- **Filter by country, category, and MOQ** — narrow results to suppliers that match your sourcing needs.
- **Country coverage** — China, Poland, Turkey, and EU countries, with new suppliers added regularly.
- **Direct contact links** — reach out to suppliers through their preferred communication channels listed on each profile.

## How It Works

1. Open the **Supplier Database** from the main navigation.
2. Use filters to narrow by country (e.g., China), product category (e.g., electronics, home & garden), or maximum MOQ.
3. Browse supplier cards showing key stats: rating, country, shipping time, and dropshipping support.
4. Click a supplier to view the full profile — including verified reviews, response time history, and platform compatibility.
5. Contact the supplier directly using the provided links or save them to your favorites list for later.
6. After working with a supplier, leave a rating and review to help the community.
