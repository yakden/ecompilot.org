## Overview

Content Generator uses AI to create ready-to-publish product content in minutes. Generate eye-catching thumbnails with DALL-E 3, write SEO-optimized product descriptions, and translate your listings into four languages — English, Polish, Russian, and Ukrainian. The built-in barcode lookup enriches your product data automatically, saving hours of manual research and copywriting.

## Key Features

- **DALL-E 3 thumbnail generation** — describe your product and get professional-quality images sized for marketplace listings.
- **SEO-optimized descriptions** — AI writes product titles, bullet points, and full descriptions with relevant keywords for better search visibility.
- **Multilingual translation** — translate listings into EN, PL, RU, and UA with marketplace-aware phrasing, not just literal translation.
- **Barcode lookup** — scan or enter EAN/UPC codes to pull product data (name, brand, category, specifications) from global databases.
- **Tone and style control** — choose between professional, casual, or premium tone to match your brand voice.
- **Bulk generation** — create content for multiple products in a single batch (Pro and above).

## How It Works

1. Open **Content Generator** from the dashboard or a product detail page.
2. Enter product details manually or scan a barcode to auto-fill basic information.
3. Choose what to generate: thumbnail, description, translation, or all three.
4. For thumbnails, describe the desired image style and composition. DALL-E 3 produces the image in seconds.
5. For descriptions, the AI analyzes your product data and competing listings to write optimized copy.
6. For translations, select target languages and the system adapts the content with marketplace-appropriate terminology.
7. Review, edit if needed, and publish directly to your connected marketplaces.
