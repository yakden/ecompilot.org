## Overview

Geo Data gives you access to a comprehensive database of 250 countries — complete with flags, currencies, capitals, and population figures — along with address validation and postal code lookup. Integrated UN Comtrade trade statistics let you research import and export volumes between countries, making it an essential tool for international sourcing decisions and cross-border selling.

## Key Features

- **250 countries database** — browse country profiles with flag, currency, capital city, population, and region data.
- **Address validation** — verify shipping addresses with postal code lookup to reduce delivery failures.
- **Postal code database** — look up postal codes for Polish and international addresses to ensure accurate shipments.
- **UN Comtrade trade statistics** — access official import/export data between countries by product category and time period.
- **Currency reference** — see current currency codes and symbols for pricing your products in international markets.
- **Country search and filtering** — find countries by name, region, or currency for quick reference.

## How It Works

1. Open **Geo Data** from the dashboard or tools menu.
2. Browse the country database or search for a specific country by name.
3. Click a country to view its full profile — flag, capital, currency, population, and region.
4. Use the **Address Validation** tab to verify a shipping address and confirm the postal code.
5. Open **Trade Statistics** and select an origin country, destination country, and product category to view UN Comtrade data.
6. Analyze import/export volumes and trends to inform your sourcing and expansion decisions.
