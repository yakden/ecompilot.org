## Overview

The Margin Calculator is a suite of four purpose-built calculators that cover the most common cost questions Polish e-commerce sellers face. From product-level gross margin to ZUS contributions, China freight, and Allegro success fees, every calculation is penny-perfect and kept up to date with current rates. The tool supports all major Polish tax scenarios including JDG, B2B, and employment contracts.

## Key Features

- **Product Margin Calculator** — enter purchase price, selling price, and costs to get net margin, gross margin percentage, and break-even quantity.
- **ZUS 2025 Calculator** — compute monthly social insurance contributions for sole proprietors (JDG), including preferential rates for new businesses, small ZUS Plus, and standard rates.
- **China Shipping Calculator** — estimate sea freight, air freight, and express courier costs from China to Poland based on weight, volume, and shipping method.
- **Allegro Fee Calculator** — calculate Allegro success fees, listing fees, and promoted offers costs by category, so there are no surprises after a sale.
- **All Polish tax scenarios** — toggle between JDG (sole proprietorship), B2B contract, and employment to compare take-home income across structures.
- **Penny-perfect accuracy** — rates are sourced from official ZUS, Allegro, and customs schedules and updated when they change.

## How It Works

1. Choose one of the four calculators from the Margin Calculator dashboard.
2. Fill in the required fields — for example, purchase price and selling price for the product margin tool, or shipment weight and method for the China shipping tool.
3. The calculator returns a detailed cost breakdown instantly. Each line item (tax, ZUS, fee, freight) is shown separately so you can see exactly where money goes.
4. Adjust inputs to run what-if scenarios — change the selling price, switch tax regimes, or compare air vs. sea freight.
5. Save or export results for use in your business plan or pricing spreadsheet.
