## Overview

Inventory Management brings warehouse intelligence to your e-commerce operation with Pareto ABC analysis, demand forecasting, and automated alerts for dead and slow-moving stock. Track every product by SKU, category, stock level, and 30-day sales performance. The system calculates holding costs and generates actionable recommendations — liquidate, discount, or bundle — so capital is never tied up in unsold inventory.

## Key Features

- **Pareto ABC analysis** — Class A products drive 80% of revenue, Class B covers the next 15%, and Class C is the long tail. Focus your energy where it matters.
- **Demand forecasting** — predict future sales based on historical velocity and seasonal patterns to avoid stockouts and overstocking.
- **Dead stock alerts** — products with zero sales in 60+ days are flagged automatically so you can take action before storage costs accumulate.
- **Slow-moving detection** — items with low sales velocity (30-59 days between sales) are highlighted for review.
- **Reorder point automation** — set minimum stock thresholds and lead times; the system notifies you when it is time to reorder.
- **Holding cost calculation** — estimate the cost of keeping each SKU in stock, factoring in storage fees, capital cost, and depreciation.
- **Action recommendations** — for underperforming products, the system suggests specific actions: liquidate at cost, apply a discount, or bundle with faster-moving items.
- **Product tracking** — organize inventory by SKU, category, stock level, and 30-day sales volume.

## How It Works

1. Open **Inventory Management** from the dashboard.
2. Import your product catalog or add items manually with SKU, category, cost price, and current stock level.
3. The system begins tracking sales and calculating velocity metrics. After a few days of data, ABC classifications and forecasts appear.
4. Review the **Dashboard** for a summary: total SKUs, stock value, dead stock count, and items approaching reorder points.
5. Drill into any product to see its sales history, ABC class, days of supply remaining, and holding cost.
6. Act on recommendations — click through to create discount campaigns, bundle offers, or liquidation listings directly.
7. Set up reorder alerts with your preferred lead time and minimum stock level per SKU.
