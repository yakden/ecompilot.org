## Overview

EcomPilot offers three plans designed to match every stage of your e-commerce journey. Start for free with core tools and upgrade when you need more power. All plans include marketplace integrations and carrier connections — no hidden fees.

## Plans

### Free — $0/month

Everything you need to get started selling on Polish marketplaces.

- 5 niche analyses per month
- 10 AI assistant queries per day
- Basic supplier search
- Margin calculator
- Basic Academy courses
- Community forum access
- Legal guides (KSeF, VAT, GDPR)
- 2 marketplace connections
- 3 carrier integrations
- Contractor verification (10 lookups/month)

### Pro — $99/month or $990/year (save 20%)

For growing sellers who need advanced tools and higher limits.

- **Unlimited** niche analyses
- **Unlimited** AI assistant queries
- Extended supplier database (200+ verified suppliers)
- 50 competitor monitoring slots
- Auto-listing creation (100 listings/month)
- Price optimizer
- Review management across platforms
- All Academy courses with certificates
- Priority email support
- 8 marketplace connections
- 7 carrier integrations
- Unlimited contractor verification

### Business — $299/month (contact sales for annual pricing)

For teams and power sellers who need maximum scale and dedicated support.

- **Everything in Pro**, plus:
- Team access — up to 10 user seats
- API access for custom integrations
- White label option
- **Unlimited** auto-listing creation
- Priority integration support
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA 99.9% uptime guarantee
- Custom analytics and reporting
- Advanced data export (CSV, API)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I switch plans at any time?**
Yes. Upgrade instantly or downgrade at the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.

**Is there a free trial for Pro?**
The Free plan is your trial — use it as long as you want. When you need more, upgrade to Pro.

**Do I need a credit card to start?**
No. The Free plan requires no credit card. You only enter payment details when upgrading to Pro or Business.

**Are marketplace and carrier integrations included?**
Yes. All integrations are included in every plan. You never pay extra for connecting a marketplace or carrier.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) and bank transfer for annual Business plans.
