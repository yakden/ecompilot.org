## General

### What is EcomPilot?

EcomPilot is an all-in-one SaaS platform built for Polish marketplace sellers. It combines niche analysis, AI-powered content creation, multi-marketplace management, logistics, supplier verification, and learning resources into a single dashboard. Whether you sell on Allegro, Amazon, or six other platforms, EcomPilot gives you the tools to find products, create listings, ship orders, and grow your business.

### Which marketplaces does EcomPilot support?

EcomPilot integrates with 8 marketplaces:

1. **Allegro** — Poland's largest marketplace
2. **Amazon** — Amazon.pl and other European Amazon stores
3. **eBay** — international reach for Polish sellers
4. **Etsy** — handmade and vintage products
5. **OLX** — classifieds and local selling
6. **Vinted** — fashion and secondhand items
7. **Empik** — books, electronics, and lifestyle products
8. **Erli** — growing Polish marketplace

### Is there a free plan?

Yes. The Free plan includes core tools like niche analysis (5 per month), AI assistant (10 queries per day), margin calculator, basic Academy courses, and community forum access. No credit card is required to get started.

## Features

### What is NicheScore?

NicheScore is EcomPilot's proprietary rating system that scores product categories from 0 to 100. It combines three signals — competition density, demand trends, and margin potential — into a single number. A higher NicheScore means lower competition, growing demand, and better margins. Use it to identify profitable niches before investing in inventory.

### What is KSeF?

KSeF (Krajowy System e-Faktur) is Poland's National e-Invoice System, which is becoming mandatory for VAT-registered businesses. EcomPilot includes KSeF integration to help you generate, send, and store structured e-invoices that comply with Polish tax regulations. Our legal guides explain the requirements and timeline in detail.

### What languages are supported?

The platform interface is available in 4 languages:

- **English (EN)**
- **Polish (PL)**
- **Russian (RU)**
- **Ukrainian (UA)**

The Content Generator can also translate product listings into all four languages for cross-border selling.

## Logistics

### Which carriers are integrated?

EcomPilot connects with 7 carriers operating in Poland:

1. **InPost** — Paczkomaty (parcel lockers) and courier
2. **DPD** — domestic and international courier
3. **DHL** — express and standard delivery
4. **GLS** — parcel delivery across Europe
5. **Poczta Polska** — Poland's national postal service
6. **ORLEN Paczka** — parcel lockers and pickup points

All carrier integrations are included in every plan. Compare rates, generate labels, and track shipments from one dashboard.

## Mobile and Security

### Is there a mobile app?

Yes. EcomPilot offers native mobile apps for iOS and Android, built with Expo (React Native). You can manage listings, track shipments, check niche scores, and respond to reviews on the go. The mobile app syncs with the web dashboard in real time.

### Is my data secure?

Security is a core priority:

- **AES-256 encryption** — all data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
- **JWT authentication** — secure token-based login with refresh token rotation.
- **GDPR compliant** — data processing follows EU General Data Protection Regulation requirements.
- **Role-based access control** — Business plan users can set granular permissions for team members.
- **Regular security audits** — infrastructure and application code are reviewed on a recurring schedule.
