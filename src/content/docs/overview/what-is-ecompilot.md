EcomPilot PL is a comprehensive SaaS platform designed for e-commerce sellers operating on Polish and European online marketplaces. It provides a unified dashboard where sellers can manage every aspect of their business — from product research and listing creation to warehouse operations, invoicing, and shipping.

## Who is EcomPilot for?

- **Individual sellers** starting out on Allegro, OLX, or Vinted who need professional tools without the enterprise price tag.
- **Small and medium businesses** scaling across multiple marketplaces and looking for centralized operations.
- **E-commerce agencies** managing multiple seller accounts and needing consolidated analytics and automation.
- **Cross-border sellers** expanding from Poland into Amazon, eBay, and Etsy across European markets.

With 18,000+ active sellers already on the platform, EcomPilot serves the full spectrum of Polish e-commerce — from side-hustle sellers to high-volume operations.

## Supported marketplaces

EcomPilot integrates with eight major marketplaces popular in the Polish and European e-commerce ecosystem:

| Marketplace | Region | Integration type |

| **Allegro** | Poland | OAuth 2.0, full API |
| **Amazon** | EU (DE, PL, and others) | SP-API |

## The 16 modules

EcomPilot is organized into 16 purpose-built modules, each addressing a specific part of the seller workflow:

1. **Niche Analysis** — Discover profitable product categories with real-time sales data, trend analysis, and competition scoring.
2. **Product Research** — Analyze individual products across marketplaces, track pricing history, and identify opportunities.
3. **Listing Manager** — Create, edit, and optimize product listings with AI-assisted descriptions and bulk operations.
4. **Inventory Management** — Multi-warehouse stock tracking with low-stock alerts and automatic marketplace synchronization.
5. **Order Management** — Centralized order processing from all marketplaces with automated status updates.
6. **Shipping & Logistics** — Integrated label generation and tracking for 7 carriers including InPost, DPD, DHL, Pocztex, GLS, FedEx, and UPS.
7. **KSeF E-Invoices** — Generate and submit structured e-invoices to Poland's National e-Invoice System, fully compliant with Polish tax law.
8. **Contractor Verification** — Verify business partners against CEIDG, KRS, and VAT whitelist registries.
9. **Financial Reports** — Revenue, margin, and profitability analytics with exportable reports and dashboards.
10. **AI Assistant** — GPT-4o-powered assistant for writing descriptions, answering questions, and generating business insights.
11. **Price Monitoring** — Track competitor pricing across marketplaces and receive alerts on price changes.
12. **Advertising Manager** — Manage and optimize sponsored listings and ad campaigns on supported marketplaces.
13. **Reviews & Feedback** — Monitor customer reviews, automate responses, and track seller reputation.
14. **Legal Knowledge Base** — 30+ guides covering Polish e-commerce regulations, consumer rights, GDPR, and marketplace policies.
15. **Team Management** — Role-based access control for team members with activity logs and permission management.
16. **Notifications & Alerts** — Configurable alerts for orders, stock levels, price changes, and system events via email and in-app notifications.

## Key value propositions

### One platform, zero tab-switching
Stop juggling between marketplace dashboards, spreadsheets, and invoicing tools. EcomPilot consolidates everything into a single workspace available in four languages: Polish, English, Russian, and Ukrainian.

### Built for the Polish market
From KSeF e-invoice compliance and CEIDG/KRS verification to InPost integration and Allegro-first design, EcomPilot is engineered specifically for the realities of selling in Poland.

### Data-driven decisions
ClickHouse-powered analytics process millions of marketplace data points to surface actionable niche insights, pricing trends, and profitability metrics in real time.

### AI that works for sellers
The built-in AI assistant does not just generate text — it understands marketplace context, Polish e-commerce regulations, and seller-specific data to deliver relevant, actionable help.

### Scales with your business
Whether you sell 10 items a month or 10,000, EcomPilot's infrastructure handles the load. The microservice architecture ensures modules remain fast and reliable as your operation grows.
