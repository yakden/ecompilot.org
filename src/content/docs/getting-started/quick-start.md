Get started with EcomPilot in under five minutes. No credit card required.

## Step 1 — Register your account

Create a free account at [app.ecompilot.pl](https://app.ecompilot.pl/register).

- Sign up with your email address or use Google OAuth for one-click registration.
- Choose your preferred language: Polish, English, Russian, or Ukrainian.
- Confirm your email address via the verification link sent to your inbox.

Your account is ready immediately — no approval process, no waiting.

> For a detailed walkthrough, see the [Create an account](/getting-started/create-account/) guide.

## Step 2 — Connect your marketplaces

Link your seller accounts so EcomPilot can sync your products, orders, and analytics.

1. Go to **Settings > Marketplace connections** in the dashboard.
2. Select a marketplace (Allegro, Amazon, eBay, Etsy, OLX, Vinted, Empik, or Erli).
3. Follow the authorization flow:
   - **Allegro** — You will be redirected to Allegro's OAuth page to grant access. No API keys needed.
   - **Amazon** — Enter your SP-API credentials (Seller ID, MWS Auth Token).
   - **Other platforms** — Follow the on-screen instructions for each marketplace's specific flow.
4. Once connected, EcomPilot begins syncing your data automatically.

You can connect multiple marketplaces and manage them all from a single dashboard.

> For step-by-step instructions, see the [Connect a marketplace](/getting-started/connect-marketplace/) guide.

## Step 3 — Start selling smarter

With your account set up and marketplaces connected, you have immediate access to all 16 modules:

- **Analyze niches** — Head to the [Niche Analysis](/features/niche-analysis/) module to discover trending product categories and underserved markets.
- **Manage inventory** — Set up your warehouses and let EcomPilot sync stock levels across all connected marketplaces.
- **Process orders** — View and manage orders from every marketplace in one unified inbox.
- **Issue invoices** — Generate KSeF-compliant e-invoices directly from your orders with a single click.
- **Ask the AI assistant** — Use the built-in GPT-4o assistant to write product descriptions, optimize listings, or get answers to e-commerce questions.
