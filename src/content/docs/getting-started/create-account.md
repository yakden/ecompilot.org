Creating an EcomPilot account is free and takes less than two minutes. No credit card is required to get started.

## Sign up

Visit [app.ecompilot.pl/register](https://app.ecompilot.pl/register) and choose one of two registration methods:

### Option A — Email registration

1. Enter your email address and choose a strong password (minimum 8 characters, at least one uppercase letter and one number).
2. Click **Create account**.
3. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link.

### Option B — Google OAuth

1. Click **Continue with Google**.
2. Select your Google account or sign in to Google.
3. Grant EcomPilot permission to access your basic profile information (name and email).
4. Your account is created and verified instantly — no email confirmation needed.

## Choose your language

During registration (or at any time afterward in Settings), select your preferred interface language:

| Language | Code |
|----------|------|
| Polish | PL |
| English | EN |
| Russian | RU |
| Ukrainian | UA |

All UI elements, notifications, and the legal knowledge base are fully translated in each supported language.

## Confirm your email

If you registered with email, you will receive a verification message within a few minutes. Click the confirmation link to activate your account.

- The link expires after 24 hours. If it has expired, go to the login page and click **Resend verification email**.
- Check your spam or junk folder if the email does not appear in your inbox.
- Make sure `noreply@ecompilot.pl` is added to your safe senders list.

## Set up your profile

After logging in for the first time, you will be prompted to complete your seller profile:

1. **Business name** — Your company or store name as displayed across the platform.
2. **Business type** — Individual seller, sole proprietorship, or company.
3. **Tax identification** — NIP (Polish tax ID) for invoice generation and contractor verification features.
4. **Contact information** — Phone number and business address (optional but recommended for shipping label generation).
5. **Default currency** — PLN is selected by default; EUR and USD are also available.

You can skip the profile setup and complete it later from **Settings > Profile**, but some features (like KSeF e-invoicing and contractor verification) require tax identification to function.
