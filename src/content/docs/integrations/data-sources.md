## Overview

EcomPilot connects to nine external data sources and verification APIs to enrich your product catalog, validate business partners, and support market research. All data source integrations are **included free in every plan** — Starter, Pro, and Business.

## Product Data

### Icecat

Access the **Icecat product data catalog** to pull standardized specifications, marketing descriptions, and high-resolution images for millions of products. When you add a product by EAN or manufacturer part number, EcomPilot can auto-fill technical attributes from Icecat.

- Product specifications and feature lists
- Marketing descriptions in multiple languages
- Product images and rich media

### UPC Database

Look up products by **barcode** (UPC, EAN, ISBN) to retrieve product names, brands, categories, and images. Useful for quickly identifying and cataloging items during inventory intake.

- Barcode-to-product lookup
- Brand and category identification
- Product image retrieval

### Open Food Facts

A free, open database of **food products** worldwide. EcomPilot uses Open Food Facts to pull nutrition data, ingredient lists, allergen information, and Nutri-Score ratings for food and beverage items.

- Nutrition facts and ingredient lists
- Allergen information
- Nutri-Score and NOVA classification
- Product images and packaging data

## Business Verification

### VIES (VAT Information Exchange System)

Validate **EU VAT numbers** in real time through the European Commission's VIES service. EcomPilot checks whether a VAT number is active and returns the registered business name and address — essential for B2B invoicing and intra-community transactions.

### KRS (Krajowy Rejestr Sadowy)

Query the **Polish National Court Register** to verify company registration data. Pull official company names, legal form, registered address, representatives, and registration dates for Polish limited companies and other entities registered in KRS.

### CEIDG (Centralna Ewidencja i Informacja o Dzialalnosci Gospodarczej)

Look up **sole proprietors and small businesses** registered in the Polish business activity registry. CEIDG verification returns the entrepreneur's business name, NIP, REGON, registered address, and activity status.

### REGON (Rejestr Gospodarki Narodowej)

Access the **National Business Statistics Registry** maintained by GUS (Central Statistical Office). REGON provides official business classification codes (PKD), registered address, and entity type for any Polish business.

## Geocoding & Trade Data

### Nominatim

**OpenStreetMap-based geocoding** for address search and coordinate lookup. EcomPilot uses Nominatim to power address autocomplete fields, validate shipping addresses, and display supplier or warehouse locations on maps.

- Forward geocoding (address to coordinates)
- Reverse geocoding (coordinates to address)
- Address autocomplete suggestions

### UN Comtrade

Access **international trade statistics** from the United Nations Comtrade database. Use trade flow data to research import/export volumes, identify trending product categories by country, and evaluate sourcing opportunities.

- Import/export data by product code (HS classification)
- Trade flow analysis by country pair
- Historical trend data for market research
