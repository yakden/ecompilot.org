## Overview

EcomPilot connects your business to eight major e-commerce marketplaces from a single dashboard. Every integration is included in every plan at no extra cost — you never pay add-on fees to unlock a marketplace. Manage listings, sync orders, and track performance across all platforms without switching between seller panels.

## Supported Marketplaces

### Allegro (Primary)

Allegro is the largest e-commerce marketplace in Poland and EcomPilot's primary integration. The connection uses **OAuth 2.0 authentication** for secure, token-based access to your seller account.

- **Listing sync** — push product listings from your EcomPilot inventory directly to Allegro, including titles, descriptions, images, parameters, and pricing.
- **Order management** — pull incoming orders into the unified order dashboard, update statuses, and trigger fulfillment workflows automatically.
- **Fee calculation** — estimate Allegro commissions and listing fees before you publish, so you can price with confidence.
- **Super Seller status tracking** — monitor your compliance with Super Seller requirements (delivery time, return rate, buyer ratings) and get alerts when any metric is at risk.

### Amazon Poland

Connect to Amazon.pl using **API key integration**. EcomPilot handles product listing creation, inventory quantity sync, and order import. Map your existing catalog to Amazon product pages or create new ASIN listings.

### eBay

Sell internationally through eBay with support for cross-border listings. EcomPilot manages currency conversion references, international shipping options, and eBay-specific item specifics mapping.

### Etsy

Reach buyers looking for handmade, vintage, and craft supplies. The Etsy integration supports listing creation with Etsy-specific attributes such as materials, occasion tags, and shop sections.

### OLX

List products on Poland's largest classifieds platform. The OLX integration handles ad creation, category mapping, and message management for local buyers.

### Vinted

Tap into the fashion resale market across Europe. EcomPilot supports listing creation with Vinted-specific fields like brand, size, condition, and color.

### Empik

Connect to Empik Marketplace, one of Poland's most recognized retail brands. Manage product listings and orders through the Empik seller API.

### Erli

Integrate with Erli, a growing Polish marketplace. EcomPilot supports product listing, order sync, and shipment updates through the Erli seller panel.

## Unified Experience

All marketplace integrations share the same workflow:

1. **Connect** — authorize EcomPilot to access your seller account via OAuth or API key.
2. **Map** — match your inventory items to marketplace-specific categories and attributes.
3. **Publish** — push listings to one or more marketplaces with a single action.
4. **Fulfill** — receive orders in a unified inbox and process them through your logistics pipeline.
5. **Analyze** — compare performance metrics across marketplaces in the analytics dashboard.

## Pricing

All eight marketplace integrations are included in every EcomPilot plan — Starter, Pro, and Business. There are no per-marketplace fees or usage-based charges for connecting your seller accounts.
