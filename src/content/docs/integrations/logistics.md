## Overview

EcomPilot integrates with seven logistics carriers to give you flexible, competitive shipping across Poland and Europe. Manage all your shipments from one dashboard — generate labels, compare rates, and track parcels without logging into separate carrier portals.

## Supported Carriers

### InPost

Poland's most popular delivery network, operating over **24,700 Paczkomaty parcel lockers** nationwide. EcomPilot supports both locker delivery and InPost courier service.

- Paczkomaty locker selection with map-based picker
- Courier delivery to home or business address
- Label generation via InPost ShipX API

### DPD

National and international parcel delivery with DPD Poland. EcomPilot supports standard, express, and pickup point options.

- Domestic next-day delivery
- International shipping to EU countries
- DPD Pickup point network

### DHL

Express and standard delivery through DHL Parcel Poland. Ideal for time-sensitive shipments and international orders.

- DHL Express for urgent deliveries
- DHL Parcel for cost-effective standard shipping
- International coverage across Europe and worldwide

### GLS

European parcel service with strong coverage in Central and Western Europe. A reliable option for cross-border e-commerce.

- Standard parcel delivery across Poland
- European network for international shipments
- GLS ParcelShop pickup points

### Poczta Polska

Poland's national postal service, offering the broadest domestic reach including rural areas. Supports registered mail, parcels, and international post.

- Widest delivery coverage in Poland
- Affordable rates for lightweight items
- International postal service

### ORLEN Paczka

Pickup and drop-off points located at **ORLEN gas stations** across Poland. A convenient option for buyers who prefer collecting parcels during everyday errands.

- Nationwide network of ORLEN station pickup points
- Easy drop-off for sellers near ORLEN locations

## Features

### Unified Tracking Dashboard

Monitor every shipment across all carriers in a single view. Each parcel displays its current status, carrier, tracking number, and estimated delivery date. Filter by carrier, status, or date range.

### Label Generation

Generate shipping labels for any supported carrier directly from the order detail page. EcomPilot pre-fills sender, recipient, and parcel dimensions from your order and inventory data.

### Rate Comparison

Compare shipping rates across carriers before choosing a service. EcomPilot shows estimated cost, delivery time, and available options (locker, courier, pickup point) side by side so you can pick the best fit for each order.

### Shipment Status Notifications

Buyers receive automatic email or SMS notifications when their parcel is shipped, in transit, and delivered. Notification templates are customizable in Settings.
